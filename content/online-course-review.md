### <a id="the-analytics-edge">[The Analytics Edge](#table-of-contents)</a>
* **Insitution**: MITx
* **Platform**: edX
* **Date Completed**:  May 28, 2015
* **[Course Website](https://courses.edx.org/courses/MITx/15.071x_2/1T2015/course/)**
* **[Certificate](https://s3.amazonaws.com/verify.edx.org/downloads/4792739100a743398f5fee3a8bc11e88/Certificate.pdf)**

This course was one of the first online courses that I ever took. It was taught by Dimitris Bertsimas who is well-known in the field of Operations Research.  The course was primarily business analytics covering fundamental methods such as linear regression, logistic regression, CART, clustering, data visualization, and linear programmnig.  The problem sets were done in R and Excel (linear programming solver).  The final course project was participating in a Kaggle competition.
