EarthScope

From January 2017 - March 2017, I worked with a good friend Sam Murphy on his StartUp called EarthScope. EarthScope aims to monitor the world's wildfires. Sam's PhD is in remote sensing and his postdoctoral research was funded by Google Earth Engine. During this time I helped him train a neural network with the U-Net architecture on his dataset of over 32 GB of satellite imagery. We used batch renormalization to deal small batch sizes. Data augmentation consisted of random crops, rotations, and horizontal flip. Our neural network produced state-of-the-art detection accuracy.
